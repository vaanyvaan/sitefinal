Fluide

Yvan Chirade, Fluide, 2025
This video is an aftermovie of the launch of "Fluide", a magazine that delves behind the scenes of the "Bloodmoon, Awakening Of Fashion" fashion show, organized and created by Estelle Schmitz and Emilie Nguyen-tu, the founders of CoutureLab, a fashion association promoting culture and art in Bordeaux. The event took place at Mono Shop in the center of Bordeaux.

I want to thank Estelle and Emilie for their trust and for the oppurtnity to capture this special evening.
