Chroma

Yvan Chirade, Chroma, 2024
Chroma stems from a personal inspiration drawn from the theme of scientific experiments and laboratories, within the framework of a short fiction film. The initial desire was to create and represent an experience with psychological and physical impacts, permeating the entirety of the body and mind. The color scheme was conceived in negative, that is, with inverted colors. The video was conceived using only red and blue.
It is composed of organic and cellular transitions and shots, thus representing the effects of injecting a substance into the body.
