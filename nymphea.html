Nymphea

Yvan Chirade, NYMPHEA, 2025
Following its latest fashion show, Couture Lab now presents Nymphea.
Nymphea is a fashion show that takes place at the Vivres De l'Art as part of the Bordeaux Electronic Week. Thirteen designers present their creations in an immersive format. This video is an aftermovie that explores what went on behind the scenes of the show, organized and created by Estelle Schmitz and Emilie Nguyen-tu, the founders of CoutureLab, a fashion association promoting culture and art in Bordeaux

This second experience with Couture Lab was very enriching to me. It allowed me to meet people from different artistic backgrounds, and gave me the opportunity to see each of them at work. I would like to thank again Estelle and Emilie again for their trust, and for this second oppurtnity, which allowed me to capture this very special event.
